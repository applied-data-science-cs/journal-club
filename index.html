For researchers around Denmark!

The Applied Data Science journal club explores recent research in Data Science and its application across industries, including engineering, physics, health, social sciences, and finance. The club aims to provide a place to discuss recent research papers. Here academics will be able to extend their knowledge, foster new ideas and form new collaborations. The club aims to provide a place for discussing research papers. Here academics can extend their knowledge, foster new ideas, and, possibly, form new collaborations. The club is intended to run from February until June 2021.

Meeting Structure:

The paper and the presenter will be announced ahead of the meeting. The idea is that all attendees read the article beforehand and come up with questions.

  1. Intro 5’
  2. Paper presentation 25’
  3. Questions & informal discussion 15’

Timetable:

We will rotate presenters among the members to give everyone a chance to present. The timetable can be found at talks.
